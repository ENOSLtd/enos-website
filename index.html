The ENOS Methodology

The 6-Cog
Diagnostic Model

Every transformation has six interlocking gears. When even one is misaligned, the whole machine slows. Our Health Check scores each cog and identifies exactly where friction is costing you.

COG 01
Strategy

Is your direction clear, communicated, and translated into actionable capability needs? Or is the vision disconnected from delivery reality?

COG 02
Prioritisation

Are your initiatives coordinated to avoid duplication and misalignment? Or are teams pulling in competing directions, draining resource and momentum?

COG 03
Delivery

Is change being executed with the rigour, pace, and quality the programme demands? Or is delivery erratic and accountability unclear?

COG 04
Governance

Is there independent oversight ensuring the programme is honest about its status? Or is the team marking their own homework — and no-one is checking?

COG 05
Adoption

Is the People Gear turning? Are your teams genuinely on board, or is silent resistance eroding the programme from within?

COG 06
Benefit Realisation

Is your strategy delivering the ROI it promised? Are benefits being tracked, owned, and realised — or simply assumed and forgotten?
